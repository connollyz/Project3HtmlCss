garden basket ( student project )

The Garden Basket logo was created in a minimalist style making it modern, yet still easily reconcilable for loyal consumers as well as inviting to new consumers of Garden Basket . The logo its self is a combination of the initials of Garden Basket (G+B) to create the look of a basket with goods inside. The feeding created by the as the mood bored suggests is organic, friendly, and has a rustic charm. The logo is easily scaled down and works in a range from black white - gray scale - two colour and full colour.
